vacation-policy.txtEmployee Vacation PolicyThe organization provides paid vacation leave to support employee well-being, productivity, and work-life balance. All full-time employees are eligible to earn paid vacation time based on their years of continuous service.Employees with less than one year of service are entitled to 10 working days of paid vacation per year. After completing one year of service, employees are eligible for 15 working days of paid vacation annually. Employees who have completed five years of continuous service receive 20 working days of paid vacation per year.Vacation time is accrued monthly and must be approved in advance by the employeeŐs direct manager. Employees are encouraged to plan vacations early to ensure operational continuity within teams.Unused vacation days may be carried forward to the next calendar year up to a maximum of five working days. Any remaining unused vacation beyond the carry-forward limit will be forfeited unless special approval is granted by Human Resources.Vacation leave cannot be substituted with cash compensation except at the time of employment termination, where applicable under local labor regulations.Employees must record all approved vacation leave in the companyŐs HR management system. Failure to record leave accurately may result in payroll discrepancies.
