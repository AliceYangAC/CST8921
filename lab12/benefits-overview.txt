benefits-overview.txtEmployee Benefits OverviewThe organization offers a comprehensive benefits program designed to support the physical, financial, and emotional well-being of employees and their families.Eligible employees receive extended health coverage, including medical, dental, and vision care benefits. Coverage begins after successful completion of the probationary period.The organization also provides life insurance and disability coverage to ensure financial protection in the event of unforeseen circumstances. Employees may have the option to purchase additional voluntary coverage at group rates.A retirement savings plan is available to help employees build long-term financial security. The organization matches employee contributions up to a defined percentage of their base salary.Employees are also entitled to wellness benefits, including access to mental health support programs, employee assistance services, and annual wellness reimbursements for eligible fitness or health-related expenses.Benefit claims must be submitted through the designated benefits portal within the specified timelines. Employees are encouraged to review benefit plan documents carefully to understand coverage limits, exclusions, and claim procedures.
