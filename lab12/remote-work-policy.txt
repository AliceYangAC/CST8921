remote-work-policy.txtRemote Work PolicyThe organization supports flexible work arrangements, including remote work, to improve employee productivity, engagement, and work satisfaction. Remote work may be permitted on a full-time or hybrid basis depending on the employeeŐs role and business requirements.Employees eligible for remote work must obtain formal approval from their manager and Human Resources. Remote work arrangements are reviewed periodically to ensure alignment with organizational goals and team collaboration needs.Employees working remotely are expected to maintain regular working hours, remain reachable through approved communication channels, and attend all required virtual meetings. Performance expectations and deliverables remain the same as for on-site employees.The organization provides necessary digital collaboration tools to support remote work. Employees are responsible for maintaining a safe and ergonomic home workspace and ensuring the confidentiality and security of company data.Occasional on-site attendance may be required for team meetings, training sessions, or important organizational events. Employees will be given reasonable notice in such situations.Failure to meet performance standards or comply with remote work guidelines may result in modification or withdrawal of remote work privileges.
